Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your products made?

Our products are ethically manufactured in facilities that we personally vet to ensure fair labor practices and quality craftsmanship. We work with partners in Pakistan, India, Italy, and Portugal who share our commitment to ethical production and exceptional quality.

What materials do you use?

We prioritize premium materials including Egyptian cotton, merino wool, silk, linen, and innovative sustainable fabrics. Each material is selected for its quality, durability, and minimal environmental impact.

How do you ensure quality?

Every piece undergoes rigorous quality control checks at multiple stages of production. Our design team personally tests samples before approving any design for production, ensuring each item meets our exacting standards.
